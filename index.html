Bourns Family Youth Innovation Center

About Our Facility

The Youth Innovation Center is the City of Riverside’s newest community center! With state of the art equipment and a mission to help propel Riverside into an innovative tomorrow, the center will offer both free and fee based activities. Our mission is to provide opportunities to visitors by introducing them to professionals and services that will promote and enhance learning to increase the likelihood of life-long success. Our focus is to prepare all Riverside residents for success by providing a safe environment that fosters collaboration, learning and creativity.

Tour Our Facility

Film Studio

The film studio, complete with vocal and recording booths, will include a green screen, cameras, lighting and everything else you need to produce your own show, short film, or soundtrack. Amateur to professional film makers and musicians are welcome to use the space for their creative endeavors. Editing software will be available to polish work.

Sound Studio

Vocal recording booths to complement the film studio. This space will make musicians fell at home to create any projects they could imagine.

MultiPurpose Room

The 1,465 square foot multipurpose room will boast makerspace capabilities with a laser cutter, 3-D printers, sewing and embroidery machines, Cricut machines, and enough work space for all your crafting and making needs. The room comes with a partition wall feature to allow for multiple activities at the same time.

Computer Lab

The computer lab is designed for 16 computer stations and will offer free computer access to visitors. Visitors are welcome to use the computers for homework, printing, emails, editing, and more. Print and copy services similar to City libraries will be available for a small fee for the convenience of visitors.

Formal Classroom

The formal classroom will be a learning space for contract classes, seminars, and workshops. 627 square feet are dedicated to learning and promoting Career Technical Education (CTE), aiding in career changes and advancement.

Lounge

Styled to be an informal classroom, the lounge is 426 square feet of space dedicated to collaboration, informal learning, group projects, and casual meetings. Visitors are welcome to sit, meet, work and play. Special features include a digital wall, game consoles and built in charging stations.

Café

A collaborative space with tables and seating, visitors are welcome to take a break and snack while working on homework, reading, or just relaxing with a beverage.

Confrence Room

Join us and reserve our conference room for all your small meeting needs! This space will be available for use for groups that need space for trainings, seminars, meetings, conference and more.

Current Classes

Virtual Art Class

Lesrn the basics with fun projects and video tutorials each week. Topics include drawing, painting and mixed media. Art kits are avalible for pick-up at the Bourns Family Youth Innovation Center at no additional cost.

Virtual Chemistry Lab

Turn your own kitchen into a labratory! Learn chemistry with fun experiments and video tutorials each week. Topics include making a lemon into a battery, using chemicals to change colors and making artificial jellyfish and seaweed. Chemisty kits are available for pick-up at the Bourns Family Youth Innovation Center at no additional cost.

Virtual Video Game Building

Blocky style coding is a way to learn major programming easily. Learn to create your own game with Microsoft Mkae Code which teaches basic concepts of both game design and programming structure.

Virtual Web Design

Students get a chance to learn to develop a website with multiple pages. They will also learn how to document and save code just like the software engineers in the field.

Earth Month Project

This is an app currently under production which wil be used for an Earth Month celebration. This app will lead people in a savenger hunt around the City of Riveride to visit other community centers.
